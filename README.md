## Amazon Sales Data Analysis

- Analyzed Amazon Brazil’s e-commerce data to uncover customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, product performance, and seasonal sales patterns that could support strategic decisions for Amazon India.
- Structured and imported a relational dataset into PostgreSQL, working with linked tables such as customers, orders, order items, products, sellers, and payments to build a complete analytical workflow.
- Applied SQL techniques including **joins, aggregations, subqueries, CTEs, window functions, ranking functions, CASE statements,** and **time-based analysis** to solve business-focused problems.
- Built customer segmentation logic to classify buyers by order frequency and identify repeat and high-value customers for retention and targeting initiatives.
- Evaluated payment behavior across order value ranges to determine the most used payment methods and understand transaction preferences.
- Analyzed product-level performance to identify top revenue-generating categories, categories with high price variation, and products with strong sales contribution.
- Studied monthly and seasonal revenue trends to highlight peak demand periods and support forecasting, inventory planning, and campaign timing.
- Tracked cumulative sales and month-over-month growth patterns to understand product lifecycle performance and long-term revenue movement.
- Converted raw relational data into actionable business insights that can inform **pricing strategy, customer retention, targeted marketing, inventory planning, and marketplace growth**.
