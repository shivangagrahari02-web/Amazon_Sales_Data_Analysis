## Executive Summary

- Analyzed Amazon Brazil’s e-commerce data to uncover customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, product performance, and seasonal sales trends, generating insights to support strategic decision-making for Amazon India.
- Built and analyzed a relational PostgreSQL database by integrating **Customers, Orders, Order Items, Products, Sellers,** and **Payments** tables to perform end-to-end business analysis.
- Applied advanced SQL techniques including **Joins, Aggregations, Subqueries, CTEs, Window Functions, Ranking Functions, CASE Statements, Time-Series Analysis,** and **Customer Segmentation** to solve real-world business problems.
- Evaluated key business metrics including **Revenue Trends, Payment Method Distribution, Average Order Value, Customer Segmentation, Product Category Performance, Seasonal Sales, Cumulative Sales Growth,** and **Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth**.
- Identified **Credit Cards** as the dominant payment method, contributing **77% of total orders**, while also leading transaction volume across low, medium, and high-value purchase segments.
- Discovered **May, July, and August** as the highest revenue-generating months, while **Spring** and **Summer** consistently outperformed other seasons, supporting demand forecasting and inventory planning.
- Revealed that **high-value customers** and **loyal buyers** contributed disproportionately to overall revenue, highlighting opportunities for loyalty programs and personalized marketing campaigns.
- Identified top-performing product categories generating the highest revenue, while categories with significant price variation indicated opportunities for premium pricing and product segmentation strategies.
- Tracked cumulative product sales and **month-over-month payment growth** using SQL window functions, enabling trend analysis of product lifecycle performance and changing customer payment behavior.
- Delivered actionable business recommendations to optimize **pricing strategy, customer retention, payment promotions, inventory planning, seasonal campaigns, and revenue growth** through data-driven decision-making.
